POOL WORLD BRANDING & VISUAL INDENTITY FOR PRINT MATERIALS

For the Pool World branding project, I was tasked with creating marketing materials that convey trust, professionalism, and a refreshing aesthetic suited to a billiards company.

I used a warm brown and utilized black as an accent to establish a warm, historic tone. The brand kit included logo concepts, flyers, and business cards, all built with consistent type and layout choices for clarity and impact.
