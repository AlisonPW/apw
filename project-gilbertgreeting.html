GILBERT, AZ GREETING 2D DIGITAL ANIMATION

This Gilbert, Arizona greeting is a personal project I created to help familiarize myself with Adobe After Effects. I developed the visual layout, typography animation, and color transitions from scratch to showcase both creativity and technique.

This piece was created in Adobe After Effects as part of my ongoing exploration into motion design and storytelling. The final animation was rendered as a high-quality MP4 and published to YouTube as a shareable project.
