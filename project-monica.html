BRIDAL SHOWER BRANDING & VISUAL IDENTITY FOR INVITATIONS

This bridal shower project involved creating invitations with a personalized brand identity for a party celbration. The goal was to design a clean, elegant, and approachable visual style that reflected Monica’s modern yet warm personality.

I developed a soft color palette, custom typography, and a minimalist logo system that could adapt across thank you cards, T-shirt design, personalized brunch napkins or custom name tags for the table setting. The final result is a cohesive, stylish identity that feels both personal and professional.
