POOL PARTY ANNOUNCEMENT DESIGN

This flyer was designed to promote a fun, family-friendly pool party event. I focused on creating a layout that is vibrant, energetic, and easy to read — perfect for a summer celebration.

I used bright, tropical colors, playful fonts, and dynamic layout spacing to communicate the lively nature of the event. The design was optimized for both print and social media formats, ensuring consistent visual impact.
